CLINICAL EVALUATION REPORT
Document Number: CER-SFC-2024-v3.1
Device: SurgiFlow Catheter
Manufacturer: MedDevice International Ltd
Date: 2024-01-10

1. DEVICE DESCRIPTION
The SurgiFlow Catheter (model SFC-200) is a sterile, single-use vascular catheter
manufactured from medical-grade polymer. It is designed for vascular access procedures
in interventional cardiology and radiology settings. The device has a proximal hub,
flexible shaft, and atraumatic distal tip with hydrophilic coating.

2. INTENDED PURPOSE
The SurgiFlow Catheter is intended to provide vascular access for the delivery of
therapeutic agents, placement of stents, and diagnostic sampling in cardiovascular
procedures. The device is intended for use in adult patients undergoing coronary
angiography, percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI), and peripheral vascular
interventions.

3. INDICATIONS FOR USE
Indicated for use in adult patients (18 years and over) undergoing:
- Coronary angiography
- Percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI)
- Peripheral vascular interventions requiring catheter-based access

4. CONTRAINDICATIONS
Contraindicated in:
- Patients with known hypersensitivity to device materials (polyurethane, nylon)
- Patients with severe peripheral vascular disease where catheter advancement may
  cause vessel damage

5. EU MDR CLASSIFICATION AND RULE
The SurgiFlow Catheter is classified as Class IIb under Rule 8 of Annex VIII of
EU MDR 2017/745. This classification is based on the device being an invasive device
intended for short-term use and intended to be connected to an active medical device.

6. NOTIFIED BODY
Conformity assessment was performed by BSI Group The Netherlands BV, Notified Body
number 2797, under EU MDR 2017/745.

7. STERILITY AND SINGLE USE
The device is supplied sterile (EO sterilisation). It is intended for single use only
and must not be resterilised or reused. Shelf life: 3 years from manufacture date.
